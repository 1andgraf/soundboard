# FL Soundboard

A sound playing app like Soundpad. Built with Electron.

## Features
1) Upload sounds and play them through your microphone
2) Effects controls (distortion, gain, pitch)
3) Censor sound button
4) Bass and high frequency sound buttons
5) Your microphone live monitoring

## Usage
1) Put your main mic in the settings
2) Put the virtual mic in the settings (You have to install BlackHole (MacOS) or VB-Cable (Windows))
3) Put your monitoring output in the settings
4) Upload your sounds and tweak the effects
